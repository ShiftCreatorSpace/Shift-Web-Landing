a collaborative space and program for curious creators

There is a wonderstruck child in all of us.
The bold, spontaneous, forever curious one
that built worlds in sandboxes.

Yet every day, “do this, don’t do that” happens.
We’re told to play it safe and strive for stability.
But progress never sits still.

That’s where Shift comes in.
This is the creator space where you can explore,
for yourself and for others.

So come and discover your knack, develop your craft.
Release your inner child and go wild.
Let’s break the rules. Let’s be tremendous.

Creator Benefits

  • 24/7 access to our space. Wifi, food & drink all day, everyday.
  • Exclusive access to speakers, workshops, and other events.
  • Fast-track for internship and job opportunities.
  • A tight-knit community of people just as crazy as you are.
